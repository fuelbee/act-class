OUR APPROACH

Reading & Writing Skill Development

ACT encompasses proven strategies which guide chilgren in the process of reading & writing skill development, involving

  • Letter sound association & word recognition
  • Basic English Vocabulary
  • Reading Comprehension
  • Mechanics of Writing
  • Direct & Embedded Grammar Practice.

English Language proficiency

ACT instills language proficiency by exposing Chilgren to level appropriate components of English language structure, which improves their ability to comprehend and apply the nuances of language in reading, writing & speaking. Our consistent exposure, practice & feedback mechanism helps them develop proficiency in English.

Blend of Whole-Language & Phonics Approach

Reading can happen through the whole-word route (instant word recognition) or Phonics route (decoding). Most of the chilgren tend to have only one of these dominant, which affects their overall fluency & decoding skills. Individualized Education Plans

ACT gives chilgren the privilege of Individualized Education Plans, which facilitate learning at a level & pace best suited for the individual. This ensures appropriate intervention for chilgren for consistent progress & productive learning, reducing stress levels.

VAKT Method of Teaching
